What we are about

Science, Technology, Engineering and Math learning for kids in a fun and real-life application way.
Science is Fun!

Our five learning centers focus on these pillars

  • Sciences
  • Mathematics
  • Environment
  • Health and Nutrition
  • Technology and Engineering
We acknowledge each child as an individual and edutain the children bearing this in mind,through age-specific language and programs.
Our learning classes are:

1.5-3 Years

Baby Steins- Focused on Sensorial learning

3-6 Years

Mini Steins-Named after Albert Einstein

7-9 Years

The Curies-Named after Marie Curie

10-12 Years

Carver Tweens- Named after George Washington Carver

How we do what we do

Little Einsteins, believes that Science Exploration in young children begins with Fun and sets the basis for a lifelong interest and knowledge of Science, technology, engineering and mathematics. Through relatable experiences and stories of `KABI’ the Science Kid and `SASHA’ the Stemette.
We teach in the below formats.

Our LAB workshop

centrally located at Lavington 891 Amboseli Road, off Convent Drive,Lavington. The Little Einssteins lab is an engaging and learning environment for our little explorers!

In-school Workshop

We offer workshops at kindergatens, primary and grade schools. Our scientists and lab Assistants bring all the equipment to engage our little explorers while in their learning instutions.

Holiday Camps

What more fun our Science Explorers have then signing up for holiday camp! We engage your children over 5 days of the holiday in Science Activities. Kabi and Sasha love the holidays

Special Events

Are you having a birthday party for your Little One? Invite our Scientists and Lab Assistants to engage the children in silly and fun Science! Edutainment has never been better! A party and learning all in one
